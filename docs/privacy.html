HEX - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

Overview

HEX is a color picker app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect — which is none.

Data Collection

HEX does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device.

  • No accounts or sign-ups required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No data sent to external servers
  • No cookies
  • No third-party advertising

Clipboard

HEX copies color codes to your device clipboard only when you explicitly tap the copy button. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

HEX does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising frameworks.

Children's Privacy

HEX does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at iliasr@me.com.
